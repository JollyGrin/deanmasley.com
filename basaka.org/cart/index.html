What happens when I order?

When you order a Basaka backpack, we schedule a bulk order with our artists in West Africa. We immediately give them the funds to buy materials and start production.

After your order, we will send you a personal thank you and add you to our newsletter to keep you up to date with what’s going on behind the scenes. Also be sure to keep in touch with our social media!

We work with our partner charities to distribute backpacks filled with school supplies to children in need! Your order helps further the education of a child trying to improve their future with education!
