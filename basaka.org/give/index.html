Give a Backpack

GIVE A BACKPACK

Even if you do not want a backpack for yourself, you can help be a part of this universal education effort. When you donate a backpack we send a free backpack to a child in need in Sierra Leone and North America. Additionally each donated backpack will be filled with school supplies!

We accomplish this through our partnerships with quality charities across the world to help distribute backpacks and maintain accountability.  In addition we work with these organizations to setup a pen pal program to encourage cultural exchange for a globalizing world.

Donates two backpacks to children in need
