Our Mission

Basaka, which means backpack in Loko, a native language of Sierra Leone, is an organization determined to empower individuals, sustain entrepreneurship, and promote universal education. We are a philanthropic organization dedicated to establishing global relationships and bettering lives. Here’s how it works: when you visit one of our vendors, you have the opportunity to buy a backpack hand-made in Sierra Leone. For every backpack purchased, we donate one backpack filled with school supplies to a student in Sierra Leone. By utilizing hand-made Sierra Leonean backpacks, we can participate in the creation of a sustainable and growable enterprise in Africa.

Daniel Bloch

Daniel is graduate of the University of Michigan in Biology. A serial entrepreneur and philanthropist he is the cofounder of nonprofit College Cryptocurrency Network, Head of Bizdev for Nairobi based startup Bitsoko, and Director of Innovation for aquaculture startup Detroit Seafood Partners.

Michael Noeske

Michael is a graduate of the University of Michigan. He firmly believes that global education offers the best opportunity for the advancement of mankind.

Stepan Vorobiev

Stepan is currently a student at the University of Toronto, where he founded the local College Cryptocurrency Network chapter. He believes that decentralized technologies will fundamentally change the way we interact with each other and the world around us. Having lived in Europe, Asia and North America, he finds the greater good in connecting people from different cultures to share and collaborate on ideas that challenge the status quo.

Dean Masley

Dean is a student at the University of Delaware, majoring in Marketing and German. Before founding the Cryptocurrency Club at UD and joining the executive board of the CCN, he used his marketing studies and experience in graphic design to help various cryptocurrency projects get off the ground.

Mustapha Cole

Director of the Sierra Leone Liberty Group, Mustapha leads charitable efforts to help grow the potential of young people and ambitious entrepreneurs in his home country.

Isa

Isa is our prominent artisan from Sierra Leone who helps create our stylish handmade packs. In addition to backpacks, Isa also makes clothing, jewelry, and a variety of other accessories.
